Conclusion & References

Conclusion

This project demonstrates the practical implementation of a VoIP call center using FreePBX, Asterisk, and softphones. Through this setup, we explored the fundamental principles and technologies of VoIP, showcasing their potential to revolutionize modern communication systems.

The steps involved in creating this system—from installing the FreePBX server to configuring extensions, IVR, and queues—highlight the flexibility and scalability of VoIP solutions. By integrating softphones such as PhonerLite, Yate, and MicroSIP, the project demonstrated how easily users can adapt to this technology without additional hardware investments.

Overall, this project illustrates how organizations can leverage VoIP technologies for cost-effective, efficient, and scalable communication solutions, whether for small-scale setups or large enterprise environments.
